Become a Student

Welcome! We are so excited that you have shown interest in becoming a student of The KVM Foundation! There are a few things you should know about us before applying to our school. First, check the following requirements to ensure you qualify for the school.

Qualifications

  1. Applicants must be in Degree College or above. Primary, secondary and intermediate students will not qualify for the KVM Institute of English.
  2. Your parents' annual income must fall below 3 Lakhs per annum.

If you fall under these qualifications we hope that you will apply to our school. KVM is a very new and unique program made for college students. Here are some details that will help you understand:

  1. We are a non-profit organization. The KVM foundation is a school that has set out on a mission to assist students just like you to improve your ability to communicate effectively in English. American teachers with one objective will only teach KVM, to engulf our students in an English atmosphere.
  2. Our curriculum will involve many interactive activities to assist you in learning English as much as possible. You will be asked to watch movies in English, listen to English music, and give presentations in English, Skype on a daily basis with students from American universities.
  3. The first KVM English course will begin on June 12th and end on Nov 14th
  4. Classes for KVM will be taught Monday – Friday, one class will be from 5:00pm – 6:30pm and the next class from 7:00pm to 8:30pm.
  5. Admission fees to a school like this would normally be 40,000 rupees. After applying to our school, if you are selected and admitted into our school, the admission fee would only be 3,000 rupees. You would be getting a 40,000 rupees value for only 3,000 rupees!
  6. If you are not selected for this batch, students are welcome to apply multiple times for our future batches.
  7. The KVM Institute of English will accept 50 students.
  8. All applications are due by APRIL 14th 2014. Any application received after this date will be denied.
  9. After all applications are collected, the KVM Foundation Board will review them. Based on certain criteria, we will select students to interview with a KVM representative in Visakhapatnam, India. After the interview process, the applicants will be reassessed and finally 50 will be notified via email and mobile phone of their acceptance. Applicants who are not selected to proceed will simply not hear back from us.
