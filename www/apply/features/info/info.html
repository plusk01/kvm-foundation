Become a Student

Welcome! We are excited that you are interested in becoming a student of The KVM Foundation! To see if you qualify to apply to our school please read the conditions and qualifications below:

Qualifications

  1. The candidates who want to apply for admission into The KVM Foundation should have minimum bachelor degree or the candidates who are studying final year degree are also eligible.
  2. Applicant’s parents’ annual income must be below 2.5 Lakhs per anum. (If candidate is selected in admission, then they have to submit the proof of original income certificate)

General Information about KVM

  1. The KVM Foundation is a non-profit organization designed, taught and staffed by Americans. The foundation is an English institution that strives to assist college-aged students in improving their ability to communicate effectively in English by immersing them in an English environment in order to help them improve their English communication skills.
  2. The KVM Foundation’s curriculum will involve many interactive activities to assist students in learning English. Students will be asked to watch movies in English, listen to English music, give presentations in English, and Skype with students from America.
  3. The KVM Institute is located at Flat No. TF-2 Ratnamala Residency, Door number 45-40-42 Akkayyapalem Main Road, Visakhapatnam.
  4. The first KVM English course will begin in January and end in April
  5. There will be two groups. Classes for each group will be taught Monday – Friday. One group’s class will tentatively be held from 11:00 a.m. – 12:30 p.m. The next group's class will be held from 1:00 p.m. to 2:30 p.m. Time will be allotted before and after class for each group to Skype with American students.
  6. Applicants who are not selected for the January-April course will be welcomed to re-apply for future courses.
  7. All applications should be sent before 17 October 2014 at 5:00 p.m. Any application received after this date will be denied.
  8. The KVM Foundation Board will review all applications. After assessing each applicant,the best-qualified applicants will be asked to return and be interviewed by a KVM representative in Visakhapatnam, India. After the interview process, all returning applicants will be reassessed. The KVM Board will make a final selection and notify applicants who are accepted via email and mobile phone.
