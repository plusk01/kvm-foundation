Dream Educate Change

KVM is a foundation that helps people live their dreams by giving them a valuable tool — an English education, which ultimately will give them the power to change their future.

Dream

The poverty cycle in India is very apparent; especially in small cities like Visakhapatnam. The idea that "I will be poor because my parents were poor" is something that is deeply engraved in the minds of people here. Children have forgotten how to dream, and the few who still do dream of just getting a good job. Forget dreaming of becoming a dancer or an actor. Survival is life for many of these people. We want to do more than just teach English; we want to inspire these people to raise their sights and to dream. This is were change begins — a simple dream.

Educate

Give a man a fish and you feed him for a day; teach a man to fish and you feed him for a lifetime. Research is showing that "giving" things away in 3rd world countries as an act of humanitarian relief is beginning to backfire. Instead of giving poor people clothes or hygiene kits hoping that people learn to help themselves, we are finding that these people have gotten used to these "free" gifts and simply wait for the next batch to come. We are not giving anything away to the people here in India. We are teaching them a skill with which they will construct their own future.

Change

We are looking for chain breakers. We as KVM understand taht we can only help those who are willing to help themselves. Although we hope that our volunteers have an unforgettable experience, our main focus is on measurably changing the lives of the people we teach. We inspire our students and give them the tools they need, then allowing them to make the changes in their lives they want to see.
